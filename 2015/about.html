About us

Cornerstone custom-designs and implements leadership development programs that help organizations transition their top talent into leadership roles. Our process helps identify potential leaders, provides a realistic job preview, helps them build skills, and affords them the opportunity to demonstrate readiness before they are promoted. We have designed programs for all types of life science organizations in the areas of sales, managed markets and medical affairs.

Cornerstone will partner with you to build an innovative process that will:
  • Identify people with the interest and potential to make transitions into different jobs/job families
  • Allow them to try on the job to confirm their interest and identify developmental opportunities
  • Provide them with coaching and feedback in the skills required for the new job
  • Evaluate their readiness to make the transition
  • Provide additional development opportunities for those who aren’t quite ready
  • Follow-up to evaluate the success of the candidates who make the transition
You can retain your top talent by preparing them for a transition from one job family to another and Cornerstone will provide you with everything you’ll need:

 Nomination and selection tools
 Realistic job preview workshops
 Self-study modules to develop basic skills
 Coaching materials
 Simulation-based evaluation programs to determine readiness for transition
 Case studies
 Inbox simulations
