More about us and Ready Day One

The Cornerstone team has significant expertise and extensive experience within the pharmaceutical and biotech industries. Cornerstone team members are accomplished leaders in their fields, with success in Field Sales, Field Sales Leadership, Medical Affairs, Commercial Sales Training & Leadership Development, as well as experts in the Organizational and Industrial Psychology arena.

Our focus in the Pharmaceutical, Biotech, and Medical Device industries enables us to be revolutionary and remain current with industry trends, customizing each program to specific client needs. Our specialization in this market sector has allowed us to partner with leading companies to identify, develop, and determine the promotion-readiness of thousands of leadership development candidates. We thrive on the success of past candidates, many of whom have risen to C-suite levels in their respective organizations. As with BI, our clients are truly partners, with relationships spanning several decades.

Cornerstone’s READY DAY ONE™ approach in developing program content is based upon a thorough job analysis of the target roles, functions and skills required for successful job performance and incorporates the core principles and best practices from industrial/organizational psychology. We recognize that the results of our programs are typically factored into client promotional decisions, and strive to create processes that are universally fair and help protect our clients from employment litigation. Thus, we insist on compliance with the Uniform Guidelines on Employee Selection Procedures set forth by the Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (EEOC), as well as the Guidelines and Ethical Considerations for Assessment Center Operations endorsed by the Society for Industrial and Organizational Psychology (SIOP).
