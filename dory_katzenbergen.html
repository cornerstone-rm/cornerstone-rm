Dory Katzenberger



Dory is an accomplished healthcare industry business executive with over 25 years of experience and results in Sales, Marketing, Program Management, Operations Management and Employee Training/Development with various organizations, including Abbott Laboratories and Boehringer Ingelheim Pharmaceuticals Inc. Dory began her career as a health care clinician and educator, allowing her to bring her expertise in project management, adult education and her solid understanding of human health and disease management to her subsequent roles,

Over the course of her career, Dory has held multiple leadership positions and has gained a reputation as an effective people leader, known for her ability to foster an inclusive and collaborative environment while driving accountability to deliver results in both U.S. and global environments. She has led teams as both a first and second line leader in Employee Training, Marketing and Market Access functions. For the past several years, her passion and her focus has been the preparation and development of leaders as a key contributor to organizational growth. She holds and MBA from the University of Connecticut and is a graduate of several leadership development programs. Dory has been recognized with numerous awards for leadership performance, sales, marketing, and operational excellence.
