Ready Day One

Cornerstone’s READY DAY ONE™ approach in developing program content is based upon a thorough job analysis of the target roles, functions and skills required for successful job performance and incorporates the core principles and best practices from industrial/organizational psychology. Inside our Ready Day One Program you will find:

  • Interactive Inbox/email simulations
  • In-depth evaluation tools
  • Skill development planning and monitoring
  • Supplementary customized coaching & video simulations
  • Interactive eLearning modules
  • Internal communication/notification tools for Candidates and Managers
  • Real-time analytics to track Candidate progress

Cornerstone custom-designs and implements leadership development programs that help organizations transition their top talent into leadership roles. Get in touch today, and let us help you move your team forward.
